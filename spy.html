THE SPY


BASIC INFO

Team Uniforms


Emblems

Health Points: 125

185 (w/ Overheal)

Speed: 107%

ABOUT

The Spy specializes in distrupting the enemy formation.
Although he is not looked up upon for his lack of contribution
in competitive play, a good Spy is a nightmare to play against
especially for unexperienced Sniper and Engineers. His main form
of attack is his knife. A backstab from his knife would instantly
kill any unfortunate soul to be in front of him. He could easily
run from any altercation by using both his disguise kit and invis
watch. Despite this, a little burn from an enemy Pyro would render
his invisibility and disguise useless, so time these right and get
to stabbing!

WEAPONS

Primary Slot

Revolver (Stock)

Ammo Loaded: 6 Bullets
Ammo Carried: 24 Bullets
Base Damage: 40

Crit Damage: 120

Melee Slot

Knife (Stock)

Base Damage: 40

Backstabs deal 6x the victim's health

Special

Disguise Kit (Stock)

Allows the Spy to disguise himself as an enemy or ally.

Invis Watch (Stock)

Allows cloaking for 9 seconds.
Cloak bar can be recharged over time or with ammo packs.

Sapper (Stock)

Slowly destroys enemy Engineer buildings.
