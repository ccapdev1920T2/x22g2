ABOUT

The Engineer is a defense class specializing in
holding objectives. His trademark PDA allows him
to create three types of buildings, including sentries:
automated guns that shoot enemies on sight, dispensers:
that provide allies with health and ammunition, and
teleporters that allow for fast travel between two
locations. Despite having all these at his disposal
enemy Spies are able to fool his sentry gun with the
use of their disguise kits, and can be able to render
his buildings useless through his sappers. Always stay
alert of your surroundings and get a Pyro to help defend
your buildings!
