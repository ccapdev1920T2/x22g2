TF2 LAUNCH MAPS

THERE ARE MANY DIFFERENT MAPS IN TF2

Each with their own unique format, however, what
some of them might have in common with others is
their map type. A map type is usually defined by
its' prefix, for example: ctf_2fort is a CAPTURE
THE FLAG(ctf) map, while cp_badlands is a CONTROL 
POINT(cp) map. I will discuss a few that you may
encounter in your first time playing the game.

CAPTURE THE FLAG

A capture the flag map is essentially what it sounds
like. You take the flag from the enemy base, bring it
over to your base and earn a point. At the same time
prevent enemies from taking your flag and doing the
same. The usual win-score is 3 points for Valve-official 
servers, however custom servers run by third parties may 
have other scores set. A prime example is the fan-favorite
2Fort. Overtime will commence once both teams have a tied
score on time-end.

CONTROL POINTS

Control point maps have control points placed on designated
spots. It is up to you, and your team to capture them all and
take the win, whilst defending your own from incoming enemies.
The two common styles of CP maps are Linear and Domination.

LINEAR

Linear style CP involves 5 control points, each team having
two control points of their own and a central, neutral control
point that either could capture. Once a point has been captured,
the control point before it would be locked, and the proceeding
point to be captured after it would then be unlocked. No more
than two points can be unlocked at a time. The team who captures
all five control points first wins. Once again, overtime will occur
if both teams have an equal amount of points controlled at time-end.
An example of a Linear CP map is cp_badlands.

DOMINATION

Unlike Linear, the less common Domination CP involves three control
points, however the twist is that ALL points are available for capture
from the get go to any point in the match. Matches do NOT enter overtime
in Domination mode, a team only wins once they capture all three points.
An example of a Domination CP map is cp_standin_final.

CUSTOM TF2 MAPS

Not all maps are made by Valve developers alone... In fact, the vast
majority of maps in TF2 are made by the community! The flashy, fun 
maps that you normally do not encounter in a Valve server usually 
have to be downloaded before you jump into a server and play.
