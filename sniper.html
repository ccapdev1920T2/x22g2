THE SNIPER


BASIC INFO

Team Uniforms


Emblems

Health Points: 125

185 (w/ Overheal)

Speed: 100%

ABOUT

The Sniper is a long range support. He can instantaneously
kill enemies between long distances. When enemies get too
close, he can use his SMG to deal with mid-to-short distance
opponents and the Kukri to rid of close quarters melee users.
Fully charged headshots with his sniper rifle can take down
even the beefiest of classes.

WEAPONS

Primary Slot

Sniper Rifle (Stock)

Ammo Loaded: 25 Bullets
Base Damage: 50-150 Non-headshot, 150 to 450 Headshot

This weapon does NOT deal random crits

Secondary Slot

SMG (Stock)

Ammo Loaded: 25 Bullets
Ammo Carried: 75 Bullets
Base Damage: 8

Crit Damage: 24

Melee Slot

Kukri (Stock)

Base Damage: 35

Crit Damage: 105
