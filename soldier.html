Team Uniforms


Emblems

Health Points: 200

300 (w/ Overheal)

ABOUT

The Soldier is an offensive class specializing in
survivability and mobility. Despite having a slow
on-ground movement speed, his ability to rocket
jump allows him to reach even the furthest and
most unlikely of spots. His health also makes him
a viable option when taking heaps of damage,
having the second largest healthpool in the game.
