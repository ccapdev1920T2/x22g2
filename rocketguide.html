ROCKET JUMPING

Q: What exactly is Rocket Jumping?

A: Rocket jumping refers to the soldier's ability to,
as the name suggests, jump with the help of his rockets.
The extra boost coming from the rocket allows him to reach
even greater heights.

Q: Is it important?

A: Yes. In order to play Soldier more efficiently, one must
know the ins-and-outs of rocket jumping. This will help you
move around the map faster and get better positioning on your
enemies, providing you and your teammates easier opportunities
to get vision on enemies, score kills and ultimately win the game.

Q: Do I need any item in particular to Rocket Jump?

A: Not exactly! You can rocket jump with ANY of Soldier's
primary weapons, ranging from the unlockables to his stock
rocket launcher. However, getting a "Rocket Jumper" (seen-
above) may be ideal to use when practicing, as this rocket 
launcher deals no damage whatsoever. (Watch your falls though).

Q: I'm all set, any videos I can get started with?

A: This tutorial by Nate Fox has been considered by many
to be really beginner friendly. Start from here and make
your way to the advanced stuff later on. Happy Rocket Jumping!
