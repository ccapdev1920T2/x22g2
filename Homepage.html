ABOUT TF2 TOOLBOX

TF2 Toolbox is a website dedicated to bringing you the
up to date information on everything TF2! Feel free to provide
your own knowledge or learn a thing or two from viewing
our player guides!

CLASSES

There are 9 classes that you may play as,
each with their own unique playstyles and abilities.

1. Offense Classes(Top Row) are fit for capturing objectives

2. Defensive Classes(Second Row) specialize in holding objectives

3. Support Classes(Bottom Row) provide help for their
team in other ways(i.e. healing, sabotage, etc.)
