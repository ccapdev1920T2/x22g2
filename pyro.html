Team Uniforms

ABOUT

The Pyro is the third and final offensive class.
He specializes in close-quarters combat, with
the use of his flame thrower. Although it doesn't
respond too well to underwater skirmishes (of which-
-there are hardly any), he also has a shotgun to help
him deal with opponents from further away. His flame-
thrower is multi-purpose, as its' gas blast allows him
to push both enemies and projectiles back. Pyro is fit
for spy-checking: a tactic to keep your ally engineer's
buildings from falling victim to enemy spies.
