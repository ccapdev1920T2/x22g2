Team Uniforms


Emblems

260 (w/ Overheal)

Speed: 93%

ABOUT

The Demoman is a defense class specializing in
bomb placement and mobility. Despite having less health
than a soldier, he can be just as deadly with proper
placement of his explosives. If coupled with proper timing,
he can both properly attack and defend objectives. However,
it takes time to fully master him, as his projectiles are not
as straight-forward as a Soldier's Rocket.
