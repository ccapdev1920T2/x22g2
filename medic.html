ABOUT

The Medic is arguably the most important support in-game.
As the Medic, your job is to heal all team mates, prioritizing
healing over damage dealing using your Medigun. Prioritize weak
and injured teammates, followed by further beefing up classes such
as the Heavy, Demoman and Soldier to strengthen your frontlines.
A Medic's best, and most important aspect is his Ubercharge, which
provides him and the one being healed temporary invulnerability to
damage. Use it wisely and heal your teammates to victory!
